Recipe for Mac and Cheese

Description:

This mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and comforting. It's insanely cheesy, so creamy, and baked with crispy breadcrumbs on top.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • Butter and Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs

How to make mac and cheese

Step 01:
Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
Step 02:
Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
Step 03:
Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
Step 04:
Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.

How long to bake mac and cheese

At 350 degrees F, this macaroni and cheese should be perfectly golden brown and gooey after about 30 minutes.
